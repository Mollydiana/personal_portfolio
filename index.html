Services

Web Design

Web Development

UI/UX

Video Editing

About me




I am a web developer and designer with a passion for art and a strong social conscience. In my past I have taught English in Spain and elementary school Spanish in the U.S. I've worked as a caregiver for people with physical and mental disabilities and as a tutor for refugees and newly resettled immigrants. Now I'm using my communication skills with the aim of creating user-experiences that are informative, aesthetically satisfying, easy-to use and helpful.
